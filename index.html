Why you should start eating kiwifruit

The health benefits of kiwifruit are underrated. Here are some of the health benefits of the kiwifruit:

Aids digestion.

Boosts immune system.

Helps prevent sickness.

Manages blood pressure.

Reduces blood clotting.

Protects against vision loss.

Helps treat asthma.

Kiwis are small fruits that pack a lot of flavor. Their green flesh is sweet and tangy. It’s also full of nutrients like vitamin C, vitamin K, vitamin E, folate, and potassium. They also have a lot of antioxidants and are a good source of fiber. Their small black seeds are edible, as is the fuzzy brown peel, though many prefer to peel the kiwi before eating it.

Kiwis have been found to aid digestion. Kiwis have plenty of fiber. They also contain a proteolytic enzyme called actinidin that can help break down protein. One study recently found that kiwi extract containing actinidin greatly enhanced the digestion of most proteins. So why arent we all eating kiwis? Kiwi fruit is enviromental friendly. Tastes great to most people. About 1% of world population is allergic. It is exciting to see the growth of people eating kiwis. Join our movement and start eating them today!
